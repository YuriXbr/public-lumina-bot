# Privacy Policy for Lumina

1. Information Collection
Lumina collects the following information:

Discord user IDs and messages for interaction purposes.
League of Legends data from Riot Games API.
2. Use of Information
The collected information is used to:

Provide and improve Lumina's services.
Generate match history and statistics.
3. Data Sharing
Lumina does not share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or to protect the rights and safety of the developer and users.

4. Data Security
The developer takes reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. User Rights
You have the right to:

Access the data Lumina has collected about you.
Request the deletion of your data.
Withdraw consent for data collection.
6. Changes to Privacy Policy
The developer reserves the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted, and continued use of Lumina constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

7. Contact Information
For any questions or concerns about these terms or the privacy policy, please contact the developer.
